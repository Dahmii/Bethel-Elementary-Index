FROM THE OFFICE OF THE HEAD OF SCHOOL

The saying “All is well that ends well” is right on our lips as we joyfully round off for the 2020/2021 academic session. We ascribe all the glory to the almighty God for peacefully seeing us through. Despite all the anxiety and apprehension associated with COVID 19, the school year turned out well. We had to explore all possible methods to help stimulate our children’s interest and curiously, so as to make it easier for them to catch up fast with lost grounds as a result of COVID 19 lock down. By the end of the session, our goals and objectives were accomplished. In the Nursery Department, our pupils improved on their identification of letters and numbers, reading and writing skills. The performance of pupils in the grade classes was amazing. They could speak and write better, confidently express themselves, and solve seemingly difficult mathematical problems. Learning outside the classroom created a lot of fun and excitement for our pupils. Our school community market day, fruit festival, music, press and dance clubs, gave them an opportunity to acquire valuable life skills and improve on their social interactions. Highly commendable is the outstanding performance of our fifth graders, the class of 2021, in external examinations to their colleges of choice, and in the mathematical Association of Nigeria maths competition, (MAN), Oyo state chapter. We urge you to keep it up. We know you will do well at college, just make excellence your focus and avoid any form of procrastination and distraction. We wish you all the best. Our invaluable parents, you have been awesome. We commend your unflinching support in all our activities throughout the session. We wish to assure you that we will not relent in our pursuit for excellence in order to make you derive maximum value for your selfless sacrifice in your children’s academic endeavor. The Bethelite 2020 and 2021 is a colourful collection of some of our programs during the two sessions that will give you a clear understanding of who we are, and what we stand for.

Why Choose Bethel Elementary?

At Bethel Elementary, we pride ourselves on providing a nurturing and inspiring environment where young minds blossom and grow. Choosing the right primary school for your child is an important decision, and we're here to demonstrate why we stand out among the rest.

We operate the Montessori method which support pupils' learning process in all curriculum areas with a blend of Nigerian and other international curricula and is carefully aligned with the International Primary Curriculum (IPC).

Holistic Education

We believe in nurturing not just academic success but also the emotional, social, and physical well-being of each child. Our curriculum is designed to foster a love for learning, creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Dedicated and Passionate Educators

Our team of highly qualified and experienced teachers are passionate about guiding and mentoring students. They're committed to creating an inclusive and supportive atmosphere where every child feels valued and motivated to excel.

Engaging Learning Environment

Our classrooms are vibrant spaces that encourage exploration, collaboration, and hands-on learning. We provide modern facilities and resources to enhance the learning experience.

Focus on Character Development

We emphasize the importance of values, character, and leadership. Our school culture promotes respect, empathy, responsibility, and resilience among students.

Extracurricular Opportunities

Beyond academics, we offer a diverse range of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, music, clubs, and community service programs. These activities help in honing talents and developing well-rounded individuals.

Parental Involvement

We believe in fostering a strong partnership between home and school. We encourage parental involvement and open communication to ensure the best support for our students.
